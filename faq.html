Frequently Asked Questions

  • How/where do you get your information? How accurate/reliable is it?The information in The Movie Shack comes from various sources. While we actively gather information from and verify items with studios and filmmakers, the bulk of our information is submitted by people in the industry and visitors like you.
  • When I try to login/register I'm told that I need to enable cookies. What are cookies? The Movie Shack uses cookies to identify registered users. A cookie is simply a piece of text that is saved in your browser's configuration to allow our servers to recognize you every time you visit The Movie Shack. Without cookies, our servers are not able to recognize you as a registered user.
  • Can you put me in touch with this celebrity?While we collect information about millions of people involved in the film and entertainment industry, we are unable to contact them or forward messages to them on behalf or our customers.
  • What is this website running on?Our site and databases are hosted on a server which has support for 4K streaming. The server itself runs on an Intel Skylake CPU with 128GB of ram along with four 2TB hard drives.
